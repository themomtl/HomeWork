Where true bread is made

Wheat is a cherished sourdough bread bakery that has captured the hearts of customers since its establishment in 2021. This artisanal bakery offers a welcoming ambiance where bread enthusiasts can savor exceptional plain sourdough loaves. With expert bakers dedicated to perfecting the traditional techniques of bread-making, Wheat ensures that each loaf boasts a delightful texture and flavor. From its humble beginnings, this bakery has cultivated a strong sense of community, providing a space where customers can enjoy the simple pleasure of a perfectly crafted sourdough bread.
